11. Common Mistakes in Motion Design (And How to Avoid Them)

Even the best designers make avoidable mistakes. Here’s how to steer clear of them:

❌ Mistake 1: Overcomplicating Animations

Problem: Too many moving parts distract from the message. Solution: Keep it simple—focus on one key motion per scene.

❌ Mistake 2: Ignoring Performance Optimization

Problem: Heavy animations crash on mobile or slow down playback. Solution: Use Motionix’s "Optimize" tool to reduce file size.

❌ Mistake 3: Poor Timing & Pacing

Problem: Animations feel too fast or too slow. Solution: Test on different devices and adjust framerate (24fps vs. 30fps).

❌ Mistake 4: Neglecting Accessibility

Problem: Some users can’t see animations (e.g., colorblind viewers). Solution: Add text captions and ensure contrast.

❌ Mistake 5: Not Testing Across Platforms

Problem: What looks great on desktop fails on mobile. Solution: Preview on all devices before finalizing.
